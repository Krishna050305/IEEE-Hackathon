Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services.

Is my health data safe?

Yes, platforms are required to follow data protection and confidentiality guidelines.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, log into your dashboard and select the appointment to reschedule or cancel.

How do I book an appointment online?

You can book an appointment online through our website by selecting your preferred doctor, date, and time. Simply fill out the appointment form and submit it.

Can I book an appointment without signing up?

No, you need to create an account to book and manage your appointments.
